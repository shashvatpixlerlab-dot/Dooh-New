# ADR 005: Content Policy (V1)

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-06-05

## Policy

Advertisers may not submit creatives that are:

1. Illegal under Indian law
2. Adult or sexually explicit
3. Hateful, discriminatory, or harassing
4. Misleading or fraudulent
5. Infringing third-party intellectual property

## Admin Process

- Approve = creative is legal and acceptable for the venue context.
- Reject = provide `rejection_reason` shown to advertiser.
- Link this policy in admin reject UI and advertiser upload flow.
